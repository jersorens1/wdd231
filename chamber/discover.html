About Last City

Welcome to the Last City Chamber of Commerce!

Established to foster economic growth and community engagement, the Last City Chamber of Commerce is dedicated to supporting local businesses and enhancing the quality of life in our vibrant city. We serve as a central hub for business networking, advocacy, and development, ensuring that Last City remains a thriving and dynamic place to live, work, and play.

Shop

Last City is home to a delightful array of popular shops that cater to diverse interests and needs. For book lovers, The Book Nook offers a cozy haven filled with new and used books, along with regular author events and book clubs.

Fashion-forward residents and visitors will find their styles at Fashion Trends Boutique, a trendy clothing and accessories store known for its unique and fashionable selections. Tech enthusiasts can explore the latest gadgets and electronics at Tech Solutions, where knowledgeable staff are always ready to assist.

Last City's shopping scene also includes unique specialty stores. From Health, to travel and beyond, you'll always find unique resources for all your shopping needs.

Restaruants

  • Gourmet Delights

    A fine dining restaurant known for its exquisite cuisine and elegant atmosphere. Perfect for special occasions and romantic dinners.

  • The Last City Grill

    A popular spot for locals, offering a wide variety of grilled dishes, from juicy steaks to perfectly seasoned vegetables.

  • Pasta Paradise

    An Italian restaurant that serves homemade pasta, delicious pizzas, and classic Italian desserts in a cozy setting.

Thank You to Our City Sponsors

We extend our deepest gratitude to the generous sponsors who continuously support the Last City community.
